An Elegant Bakery Story

Welcome to Vanilla Beans, a premium artisanal bakery nestled in the heart of Kolkata. Born out of a deep passion for baking and an eye for aesthetics, our women-owned kitchen crafts more than just desserts—we create edible art.

Every cake, cheesecake, and cookie is meticulously handcrafted using the finest ingredients, ensuring a luxurious experience in every single bite. From custom celebration cakes to everyday cravings, we bring warmth and sweetness to your special moments.
